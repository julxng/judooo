## 1. Brand Voice & Positioning

### Positioning Statement

Judooo is the local art companion for Vietnam — not a gatekeeping institution, not an influencer account, but a practical tool that makes discovering and attending art events feel open, easy, and exciting for anyone curious enough to show up.

### Voice Characteristics

| Trait | What it sounds like | What it does NOT sound like |
|---|---|---|
| **Approachable** | "This weekend's lineup has something for everyone — from first-timers to seasoned collectors." | Academic jargon or elitist art-speak |
| **Locally grounded** | Naming specific districts, streets, and neighborhoods (Quan 1, Tay Ho, Dong Da) | Generic "Vietnam" or vague geography |
| **Informative-first** | Lead with what, where, when, why go — then the platform plug | "Download our app!" as the headline |
| **Quietly confident** | Sharing real data: "42 exhibitions happening this month in HCMC" | Hyperbolic claims or "the best art platform ever" |
| **Bilingual-natural** | Vietnamese as the primary voice, English seamlessly woven in — not an afterthought | Stiff, Google-Translate-quality English translations |

### Bilingual Approach

Every post is published in both Vietnamese and English. The structure follows this pattern:

- **Vietnamese first** (primary audience, larger reach on Facebook)
- **Line break / divider** (use --- or a simple line gap)
- **English second** (expat community, international collectors, SEO value)

Vietnamese captions should feel natural and conversational — written by someone who lives in Vietnam, not translated from English. English captions should carry the same tone without being a word-for-word translation.

### Brand Personality on Each Platform

- **Facebook**: Community hub. Informative, event-forward, conversational. This is where the full event listings, discussions, and gallery partnerships live. Lean into Facebook's strength as a local events and community platform — 94% of Vietnamese internet users are on Facebook.
- **Instagram**: Visual portfolio. Curated, aesthetically sharp, discovery-driven. This is the window into the art itself. Lean into the fact that Instagram users in Vietnam tend to be more affluent and aesthetically oriented — ideal for reaching collectors and design-conscious audiences.

## 2. Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Event Discovery ("What's On" / "Tuan Nay Co Gi")

**Purpose**: Drive traffic to judooo.com event listings
**Share of content**: 35%

Content under this pillar:
- Weekly event roundups for HCMC and Hanoi
- Individual event highlights (exhibitions, workshops, auctions)
- "Last chance" reminders for events closing this week
- New event announcements sourced from the Judooo directory
- Free vs. paid event callouts

**Why it works**: This is Judooo's core utility. Every event post is a reason to visit the website. The goal is to become the default answer to "What art events are happening this weekend?"

### Pillar 2: Art & Artist Spotlights ("Behind the Work" / "Cau Chuyen Nghe Thuat")

**Purpose**: Build emotional connection and position Judooo as a platform that values artists
**Share of content**: 25%

Content under this pillar:
- Artist profile features (studio visits, process shots, short interviews)
- Artwork close-ups from the Judooo marketplace
- Gallery/space profiles (The Factory, Manzi, Galerie Quynh, A.Farm, etc.)
- "Meet the maker" posts connecting artists to their listed works
- Collector stories: "Why I bought this piece"

**Why it works**: Art content is inherently visual and shareable. These posts serve both artists (who share them with their networks) and collectors (who discover new work). They also feed the marketplace funnel — every artist spotlight can link to their artworks on judooo.com.

### Pillar 3: Art Education & Culture ("Art Made Simple" / "Nghe Thuat Cho Moi Nguoi")

**Purpose**: Lower the barrier to entry for new art audiences
**Share of content**: 20%

Content under this pillar:
- "How to visit a gallery for the first time" guides
- Art terminology explained simply (bilingual glossary posts)
- "What to look for" at different event types (auction etiquette, workshop prep)
- Vietnam art history bites (Dong Ho prints, lacquer painting tradition, Indochina Fine Arts College legacy)
- Art market trends and collecting basics
- Route planner feature tutorials ("Plan your Saigon art weekend in 3 taps")

**Why it works**: The biggest barrier to art participation in Vietnam is intimidation, not interest. Educational content expands the total addressable audience and aligns with Judooo's mission of making art accessible.

### Pillar 4: Community & UGC ("Your Art Weekend" / "Cuoi Tuan Nghe Thuat Cua Ban")

**Purpose**: Build community, generate social proof, and encourage platform engagement
**Share of content**: 15%

Content under this pillar:
- User-submitted photos from events they discovered on Judooo
- "Tag us at the gallery" campaigns
- Polls and questions ("Which exhibition are you checking out this weekend?")
- Reposting stories from users, galleries, and artists who tag Judooo
- Community milestones ("500 events listed on Judooo!")

**Why it works**: UGC creates a flywheel — people who see others attending events are more likely to attend themselves. It also gives galleries and artists a reason to mention Judooo organically.

### Pillar 5: Platform & Product ("Built for Art Lovers" / "Xay Dung Cho Nguoi Yeu Nghe Thuat")

**Purpose**: Drive feature awareness and website traffic
**Share of content**: 5%

Content under this pillar:
- New feature announcements (route planner, marketplace updates, watchlist)
- "Did you know?" tips about Judooo functionality
- Before/after comparisons ("Planning your gallery visits used to take 30 minutes...")
- Testimonials from galleries and artists using the submission pipeline

**Why it works**: Keep this pillar lean. Nobody follows a social account to see product updates. But occasional, well-crafted product posts convert followers into active users.

## 3. Posting Frequency

### Facebook

| Content Type | Frequency | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Feed posts (events, spotlights, education) | 5x per week | Facebook's algorithm rewards consistent posting. Vietnam's Facebook audience expects regular updates from pages they follow. |
| Stories | 3-5x per week | Stories maintain top-of-feed presence. Use for quick event reminders, polls, countdowns. |
| Facebook Events (co-created with galleries) | As available | Co-creating Facebook Events with partner galleries drives direct RSVPs and shows up in users' event feeds — a powerful discovery channel. |
| Reels | 2x per week | Facebook is pushing Reels hard. Short video content gets disproportionate reach. |

**Total**: ~5 feed posts + 2 Reels + 3-5 Stories per week

### Instagram

| Content Type | Frequency | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Feed posts (carousel, single image) | 4x per week | Quality over quantity on Instagram. Each post should be visually polished. |
| Reels | 3x per week | Reels are Instagram's highest-reach format. Art content (process videos, gallery walkthroughs) performs exceptionally well. |
| Stories | Daily (5-7x per week) | Stories are the heartbeat of Instagram engagement. Polls, questions, countdowns, and reposts keep the account active. |

**Total**: ~4 feed posts + 3 Reels + daily Stories per week

### Optimal Posting Times (Vietnam — ICT / UTC+7)

| Time Slot | Platform | Content Type |
|---|---|---|
| 7:00-8:00 AM | Facebook | Morning event roundups, educational content |
| 11:30 AM-1:00 PM | Facebook & Instagram | Lunch-break content (carousels, artist spotlights) |
| 7:00-9:00 PM | Instagram & Facebook | Evening posts (Reels, event highlights, weekend previews) |
| Thursday 7 PM | Both | Weekend preview posts (highest intent to plan) |
| Saturday 10 AM | Both | Weekend event reminders |

## 5. Content Formats by Platform

### Facebook Formats

| Format | Use Case | Specs | Tips |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Photo post (single image)** | Event announcements, artwork features | 1200x630px or 1080x1080px | Always include event date, location, and judooo.com link |
| **Photo album** | Multi-event roundups, gallery visit recaps | 3-10 images | First image should work as standalone; caption each photo |
| **Link post** | Driving traffic to judooo.com | Auto-generates preview card | Write a compelling caption; don't rely on the link preview alone |
| **Video/Reel** | Gallery walkthroughs, artist process, event vibes | Vertical 9:16, 15-60 seconds | Hook in first 3 seconds; add Vietnamese subtitles |
| **Facebook Event** | Co-created with partner galleries | Event cover 1920x1005px | Include Judooo as co-host; link to event detail on judooo.com |
| **Stories** | Polls, countdowns, quick updates | 1080x1920px | Use interactive stickers (poll, quiz, countdown to event) |
| **Text post** | Community questions, discussion starters | No image | Keep under 150 words; ask a clear question |

### Instagram Formats

| Format | Use Case | Specs | Tips |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Carousel (up to 20 slides)** | Event roundups, artist features, educational series | 1080x1350px (4:5 portrait) | Slide 1 is the hook. Last slide is the CTA ("Save this for the weekend"). Use consistent template design. |
| **Single image** | Standout artwork, gallery interior, event poster | 1080x1350px (4:5) or 1080x1080 (1:1) | Clean, high-quality photography. Minimal text overlay. |
| **Reel** | Gallery walkthroughs, artist process, event atmosphere | Vertical 9:16, 15-90 seconds | Trending audio (Vietnamese music when possible). Text overlays for silent viewing. First frame must grab attention. |
| **Stories** | Daily updates, polls, countdowns, reposts, behind-the-scenes | 1080x1920px | Use location tags (gallery/district names). Poll stickers for engagement. Link sticker to judooo.com. |
| **Story Highlights** | Permanent categorized content | Custom cover icons | Categories: "This Week" / "HCMC" / "Hanoi" / "Artists" / "How To" / "Marketplace" |
| **Guides** | Curated collections | Existing posts grouped | "Best galleries in District 1" / "First-time collector guide" |

### Visual Style Guide

- **Photography**: Natural light, clean composition, emphasize artwork and gallery spaces. Avoid heavy filters.
- **Graphics/Templates**: Use Judooo's design system — black/white/neutral palette with the Inter font family. Let the art provide the color.
- **Carousel templates**: Consistent header bar with Judooo branding. White or off-white backgrounds. Art images as hero elements.
- **Reel style**: Steady camera work (gimbal recommended for walkthroughs). Subtitles in Vietnamese with English below. Minimal transitions — let the art and spaces speak.

## 7. Growth Tactics for the Vietnam Art Scene

### Tactic 1: Gallery & Space Partnerships

**What**: Establish content partnerships with 10-15 key galleries and art spaces in HCMC and Hanoi.

**Target partners**:
- HCMC: The Factory Contemporary Arts Centre, Galerie Quynh, San Art, Craig Thomas Gallery, Dia Projects
- Hanoi: Manzi Art Space, Art Vietnam Gallery, VCCA (Vincom Center for Contemporary Art), The Outpost, Nha San Collective, APD (Center for Art Patronage and Development)

**How it works**:
- Judooo lists their events (they already do via the platform)
- Galleries tag @judooo when posting about events; Judooo reposts
- Co-create Facebook Events for major openings
- Provide galleries with a "Listed on Judooo" badge graphic for their own posts
- Monthly cross-promotion: Judooo features partner gallery; gallery shares Judooo's weekly roundup

**Growth impact**: Each gallery has its own follower base (typically 2K-30K). Cross-tagging creates mutual discovery.

### Tactic 2: "Listed on Judooo" Badge for Event Organizers

**What**: Create a simple, clean social graphic that galleries and event organizers can add to their own promotional posts indicating the event is listed on Judooo.

**Why it works**: It turns every partner's post into a referral for Judooo — organic, scalable, and free.

### Tactic 3: Facebook Group Seeding

**What**: Participate actively in existing Vietnam art and culture Facebook groups. Do not spam. Add genuine value.

**Target groups**:
- Vietnam Art Space
- Expat groups in HCMC and Hanoi (large, active, always asking "what to do this weekend")
- Photography and creative communities
- University art department groups (Ho Chi Minh City University of Fine Arts, Vietnam University of Fine Arts in Hanoi)

**Approach**:
- Share the Monday "What's On" roundup as a helpful resource, not a promotion
- Answer questions about art events with links to judooo.com event pages
- Build reputation as the most informed voice about art happenings

### Tactic 4: Collaborate with Art & Culture Media

**What**: Build relationships with Hanoi Grapevine, Saigoneer, Vietcetera, and The Hanoian for content cross-promotion.

**Why**: These platforms already serve the art-curious audience in Vietnam. Hanoi Grapevine in particular operates as a bilingual arts and culture platform with a very similar audience.

**Approach**:
- Offer Judooo event data for their weekly roundups (make their job easier)
- Guest-contribute event roundup posts to their platforms
- Co-host Instagram Lives or Facebook Live sessions before major gallery weekends

### Tactic 5: Artist Amplification Program

**What**: Feature emerging Vietnamese artists on Judooo's social channels with the explicit goal of helping them reach new audiences.

**Why it works**: Artists share content that features them. Every artist feature post gets shared to the artist's personal network, exposing Judooo to a new audience. Additionally, this positions Judooo as a platform that genuinely supports artists, not just aggregates them.

**Format**:
- Instagram carousel: 5 slides (artist photo, 3 artworks, Q&A slide with bio + link to their Judooo marketplace page)
- Facebook: Long-form interview post with images
- Artist receives a "Featured on Judooo" highlight graphic to share

### Tactic 6: University & Art School Outreach

**What**: Connect with art students and faculty at Vietnam's major art schools.

**Targets**:
- Ho Chi Minh City University of Fine Arts
- Vietnam University of Fine Arts (Hanoi)
- RMIT Vietnam (Design & Art programs)
- Hue University of Arts
- British University Vietnam (BUV) creative programs

**Approach**:
- Offer to list student exhibitions for free (they already can via Judooo's submission pipeline)
- Run a "Student Spotlight" series on social media
- Partner with student organizations for gallery visit events (using Judooo's route planner)

### Tactic 7: Event-Day Live Coverage

**What**: Attend 2-3 key openings per month and provide real-time Instagram Story and Facebook Story coverage.

**Why**: Live, on-the-ground content is the most engaging and the hardest to replicate. It builds trust that Judooo is actually embedded in the scene, not just aggregating from a desk.

**Approach**:
- Stories: Quick clips of the space, crowd, standout works
- Tag the gallery, artists, and attendees
- End each Story series with a "See all events this week" link sticker to judooo.com

### Tactic 8: Seasonal Campaigns Tied to Vietnam's Cultural Calendar

| Season/Event | Campaign Angle | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| **Tet (Lunar New Year)** | "Art gifts for Tet" + Tet-themed exhibitions roundup | January-February |
| **International Women's Day** | Feature female Vietnamese artists + women-led galleries | March 8 |
| **Vietnam Heritage Day** | Vietnam's traditional art forms (lacquer, silk, Dong Ho prints) | November 23 |
| **Mid-Autumn Festival** | Art workshops for families, lantern-making events | September |
| **Gallery Weekend** | Self-organized quarterly event encouraging gallery crawls in a single district | Quarterly |
| **Year-End Auction Season** | Collector-focused content, auction previews, bidding guides | November-December |

### Tactic 9: Instagram Reels Series with Signature Formats

Create 3 recurring Reels series that become recognizable:

1. **"60 Giay Tai Gallery" (60 Seconds at the Gallery)**: Quick walkthrough of a gallery or exhibition. Steady gimbal shot. Vietnamese music. Subtitles with event name, dates, address, and "Listed on Judooo."

2. **"Truoc vs Sau" (Before vs After)**: Split-screen showing an artist's workspace/process alongside the finished piece. Works beautifully as a 15-second Reel.

3. **"Ban Da Biet?" (Did You Know?)**: Quick art facts about Vietnam's art history. Text-on-screen format. One fact per Reel. Example: "Son mai (Vietnamese lacquer art) takes up to 6 months for a single piece. Here's why."

### Tactic 10: Paid Amplification Strategy

**Monthly ad budget allocation** (suggested starting budget: $300-500/month):

| Channel | Budget % | Objective | Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Facebook feed ads | 40% | Event awareness + website traffic | 25-45 year olds in HCMC & Hanoi interested in art, design, culture |
| Instagram Reels ads | 30% | Reach + follower growth | 22-40 year olds, interest in contemporary art, galleries, interior design |
| Facebook Event promotion | 20% | RSVPs for partner gallery events | Geo-targeted to event city, 7-day window before event |
| Retargeting | 10% | Re-engage judooo.com visitors | Website visitors who viewed 2+ events but didn't save/register |

## Appendix: Competitive Landscape

Understanding what already exists in the Vietnam art social media space helps Judooo find its unique angle.

| Account/Platform | What They Do | Judooo's Differentiation |
|---|---|---|
| **Hanoi Grapevine** (@grapevine.hanoi) | Bilingual arts & culture event listings for Hanoi. ~6K IG followers. | Judooo covers all of Vietnam (not just Hanoi) and adds interactive features (save, plan routes, marketplace). |
| **Saigoneer** (@saigoneer) | Broad culture/lifestyle media for HCMC. Art is one of many topics. | Judooo is art-only and deeper — not a media outlet but a utility platform. |
| **The Factory** (@thefaboratory) | Single gallery's own account. | Judooo aggregates ALL galleries and events — not tied to one space. |
| **Vietcetera** (@vietcetera) | Vietnamese culture media (English). Art is occasional. | Judooo is art-specific with a functional web tool behind the content. |
| **Individual artist accounts** | Artists promote their own work. | Judooo amplifies artists AND gives them marketplace + event submission tools. |

**Judooo's unique position**: No other social account in Vietnam combines art event aggregation, marketplace access, and a functional web tool. Every post can link to something actionable on judooo.com — not just content for content's sake.
